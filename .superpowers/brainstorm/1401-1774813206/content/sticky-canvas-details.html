Key question: How should the text content overlay?

The particle canvas is sticky. Text scrolls on top. How do we handle readability?

A

Left-aligned text cards

Text stays on the left (like current hero). Particle canvas shifts its center-of-gravity to the right as sections progress. Clean separation — text never fights the visuals.

B

Centered text with backdrop blur panels

Each pipeline step appears as a frosted glass card in the center. The particle canvas is behind it but visible around the edges. More dramatic but text could clash with bright particles.

C

Alternating left/right

Steps alternate sides — Step 1 text on left, Step 2 text on right, etc. Camera shifts to the opposite side each time. Creates a zig-zag reading flow that feels dynamic.
